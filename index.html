This a webpage for you to learn Chinese through singing Chinese songs

I found a place where I can practice my Japanese by singing Japanese songs, I love all the features that website has and I want to create one for Chinese learner too.

Language you can change include:

  • Chinese Mandarin
  • Chinese Cantonese
  • Korean
  • Japanese

What you need to do to get started

  1. Go to home
  2. Choose a song
  3. Click the play button
  4. Sing!!
